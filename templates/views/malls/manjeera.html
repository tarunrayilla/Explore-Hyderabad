About

Manjeera Trinity Mall, a shopping extravaganza in Hyderabad and a venture by the Manjeera Group, brings to you a refreshing combination of brands, entertainment, food and cultural aesthetics, making it truly a ‘hub of good times’. The mall consists of 6 levels for shopping and 4 levels of parking built on a total space of 4,50,000 sq. ft. Manjeera showcases the best of brands including Shoppers Stop, Cinepolis, Mc Donalds, KFC, Subwayand more.
