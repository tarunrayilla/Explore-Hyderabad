About

Chill and Terrace is the buffet restaurant at Radisson Blu. As the name suggests, the restaurant boasts of an al fresco dining area in addition to the spacious and comfy but utilitarian dining hall. A la carte selection is available round the clock and is accompanied by buffet during breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Sundays at Radisson are a busy affair owing to the lavish brunch spread across all 3 of its FnB outlets. The buffet spread is fairly small, but the food is dependable. The live kitchen adds a touch of flare to your dining experience and doubles up as a live station. The food menu follows the standard template and offers a mix of local flavours, North Indian favourites, and popular Western dishes such as 'Fish and Chips' and 'Lamb Chops'. The North Indian fare is especially noteworthy, and the 'Rogan Josh' here is head and shoulders above most of the other renditions available in the city.
