About

Hyderabadi and biryani are synonymous and Paradise Hotel is a top priority on every Hyderabad visitor’s to-do list. Paradise Circle is where the world comes to have its most popular share of Hyderabadi dum biryani, kebabs and haleem especially during the month of Ramzan. But that’s not all. Located on M.G.Road, one of the busiest roads of the twin cities Hyderabad and Secunderabad, this is also a foremost shopping district in Secunderabad. Popular haunts for shoppers and bargain hunters, stores of numerous global brands, this neighbourhood has been flourishing for over 150 years and today is filled with bazaars, markets with a variety of local and street food to give company
