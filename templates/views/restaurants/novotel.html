About

The Square, the coffee shop at Novotel Hyderabad Airport, is mainly known for its sumptuous buffets and is popular among airport staff and airline crew from the airport apart from in-house guests. The restaurant has a modern look with comfortable indoor as well as al-fresco seating. Their Sunday Brunch is a busy affair where many drive down from core areas of Hyderabad for a weekend outing with their families to spend a lazy afternoon here. One can enjoy from a huge menu of food and drinks, along with a quick dip at the pool (complimentary with the brunch), with a live band to entertain. The dessert counter is particularly enticing with a variety of local and international delicacies with a beautiful presentation. The weekend dinners often have special promotions such as live barbeque grills. Head towards The Square this Sunday and relish a mouth-watering spread of food that shall only leave you wanting for more. Enjoy the day off with your loved ones with great food coming your way. Be there and pamper your taste buds with delicious food accompanied by great ambiance that is sure to make the day off worth remembering!
