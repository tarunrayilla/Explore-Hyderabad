About

Why go to Shimla, Nainital or any other hill-station in India when you can witness the snow in Hyderabad itself? Also known as the first snow-themed park in the country, the Snow World gives a chilling experience to the locals and tourists alike and is undoubtedly a must-visit attraction for all those who want to have fun in the snow. Attracting people from different aspects of life, the Snow World has various games and activities to keep everyone’s interest glued. A few of them include snow basketball, snow volleyball, ice skating, ice boarding and snow dancing. Adding to these are snow slides, snow rides, snow sculptures and snow landscapes that are made of artificial snow. There is also a dedicated snow play area where you can leave your kids without any worries or tension.
