About

Elles World, Amusement and Theme Parks in Hyderabad Last but not the least, Elles World is one another amusement and theme park in Hyderabad that is well-equipped with a plethora of regular and water rides. A go-to place for all age groups, Elles World is situated near to Ocean Park and has something interesting to offer for everyone. Visit Elles World to enjoy a quick break from the normal routine at a pocket-friendly price. This amusement park entertains both adults and kids and has a huge foot court where you can discover the food flavour after your time at the adventurous activities.
