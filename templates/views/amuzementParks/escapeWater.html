About

Have the best time of your life at Escape Water and Amusement Park in Hyderabad. This unspoiled site boasts of an array of high-thrill water rides that gives you a much-needed break from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. It is a great place for summer outings and an ideal location to create memories for a lifetime! From stimulating sports to a plethora of adventurous activities, there are several things to keep you occupied throughout the time. Jumbo Raft, Super Cambo, Cracker Curves, Race Bowl, Tilting Bucket, Blue Bang and Float Race Tunnel are a few of the crazy rapids to name. Besides this, the adventure park also offers customized corporate packages for a minimum of 100 people.
