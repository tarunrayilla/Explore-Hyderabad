About

Meant for adventure junkies, the Mount Opera Theme Park in Hyderabad, nestled just a short stroll away from Ramoji Film City, promises to leave you mesmerized with an unparalleled range of leisure and recreational activities. The resort allows you to have a wonderful time away from the hustle and bustle of city life. With a startling choice of mechanical rides, from Jumping Frog to Merry Cups, Merry Go Round, Ferris Wheel, Tele Combat, Bungee Jumping and Thrillarium, you can keep your excitement level high throughout the day. There is a Water World with a Wave Pool, Typhoon Tunnel, Zip Zap Zoop, Blue Lagoon-Family Pool, Rain Dance Floor and Water Slides. If you don’t want to get wet, you could pay a visit to the indoor section to play indoor games such as carom, chess, pool table or table tennis.
