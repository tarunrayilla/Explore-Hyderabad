About

Wild Waters Amusement & Water Theme Park, Amusement and Theme Parks in Hyderabad If you’re looking for unlimited fun and boundless entertainment, then there is no place better than Wild Waters Amusement & Water Theme Park. Presenting a varied range of thrill rides, kids rides and family rides, the Wild Waters Amusement & Water Park is perhaps the biggest theme park with 60 plus dry and wet rides in Hyderabad. Catering to everyone’s needs, the amusement park gives you a chance to enter into the world of drama and adventure with best-in-class action that cannot be seen anywhere else. It has 21 rides in aqua park, 17 rides in adventure park and 22 rides in the amusement park. All rides have been carefully designed keeping the safety requirements in mind and provides an experience worth-remembering.
