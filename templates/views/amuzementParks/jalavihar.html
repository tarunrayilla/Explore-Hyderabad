About

Sprawling over an area of 12.5 acres of land, Jalavihar Water Park is another biggest amusement and theme parks in Hyderabad that introduces you to a next-level of water games and other slides under one roof. It is an idyllic holiday destination for a full-day excursion and can be visited by solo travellers, couples and families with children. An entertaining place with an innumerable number of dry rides, the Jalavihar Water Park allows you to dance to the tune of your favourite chartbuster at the Rain Dance Shower. Here you can enjoy a dip in the swimming pool or please your senses at the Wave pool. For ladies, there is a separate pool and for little guests, there is a dedicated water play area with fun games. Other than that, the fun park in Hyderabad features an amazing amphitheatre and a party zone for organizing birthday parties, social gatherings and corporate lunches.
