About

The next one-of-its own kinds of resorts in Hyderabad is Pragati Resorts, which is a premium destination to enjoy a relaxing stay at budget-friendly prices. Surrounded by dense greenery and sprawling lawns, the Pragati Resorts offers a tranquil setting to serve its patrons with best-in-class hospitality. It is located within easy reach of Chilkur Balaji Temple and can be easily accessed by local modes of transport such as private taxi and Pushpak Bus. From Lagoon Rooms to executive cottages and signature suites, this resort is bestowed with an accommodation that offers a luxurious stay with an aim of decluttering your minds. Featuring an abundance of nature with ultimate comfort, the rooms are designed keeping the warmth factor in mind.
